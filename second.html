Frequently Asked Questions

Ask whatever you want to ask about FAF

  • Does the Coach you get have qualified skills?

    All personal trainers we have are internationally accredited and have more than 5 years of experience in the world of fitness.

  • can we save video tutorial?

    For now the Video Tutorial service can only be viewed online, but FAF plans to improve the service by providing a video storage feature for those of you who subscribe to the Beginner package.

  • What information is in the health consultant feature?

    In the Health Consultant feature, there is a variety of information about exercise health which is attached to the articles related to the FAF version. In addition, we also plan to add a Chat feature so that users can consult directly with health experts who work with FAF.

  • What features do I get if I subscribe to FAF?

    There are so many features that you can get if you subscribe to FAF. Starting from Training, Tutorials, Store, Nutrition Consultant, and Health Consultant. For more details, you can see the "Offer" section of the FAF website page.

  • Can the subscription plan be changed?

    The subscription package can be changed at the end of the previously selected period. The period in question is 1 month (30 days) from the purchase of the previous subscription package and the payment has been paid off at a predetermined period of time.
